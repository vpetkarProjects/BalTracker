Income, Expense and Balance tracker  :

Project to keep track of income and expenses. 

To add a Income you need to give a positive number (for eg 100 or +100) and for expense you need to give negative number (for eg -100).

It will display each +ve or -ve transaction in History. 

The total of Income and total of Expenses is displayed in Income/Expense table

Balance amount is displyed above the table.
